Senda Athletics

A bay-area startup to sell futsal balls, Senda was started with a clear goal: disrupt the industry by embracing Fair Trade, bringing back craftsmanship, promoting joyfully playing, and improving lives along the way. Senda works to turn the current business model on its head. The model of major brands paying players millions for endorsements, while going to the developing world and making factories compete to offer them the lowest price (often at the expense of workers) is a broken model that needs to be changed.

LifeWork Holdings Inc.

We are on a mission to make remote, freelance work a sustainable, inclusive career choice. We see a world where everyone gets to work as they choose. Your hours. Your workplace. Your rules.No more finding clients, filing expenses, and chasing payments. In our future, we only do enjoyable, appropriate, and meaningful work. LifeWork is the freelance work platform that puts you, the freelancer, first. We believe the biggest problem in freelancing is income insecurity. Insecure income robs us of our flexibility - the reason we became freelancers! We’re going to employ freelancers to give them flexicurity.
